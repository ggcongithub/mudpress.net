Creator Collaboration Plan

Mudpress selects works it admires for printing, packaging, and selling. After deducting the costs of printing and other production expenses, the remaining profit is shared 55% with the author (the author does not have to pay any fees).

The number of prints is determined through negotiation. This method of cooperation can only be sold on Mudpress's platform, and the work needs to bear the Mudpress logo and name in a prominent location. (You can also submit your works to our email mudpress@163.com or contact us directly.)
