why "mud" press

When you step on the ground, if there is mud under your feet, walking leaves a lot of traces. It's as if the earth is paper, shoes are the printing plate, and the mud is the ink.

In this way, wherever a person walks, they are creating something.

About MUDpress

In 2020, MUDpress was established in Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China.

We are committed to cultivating a Risograph youth culture community, sharing, promoting, and popularizing printing and binding craftsmanship. This allows each uniquely creative individual to express themselves while seeing and connecting with each other.

what mupress do

We undertake Riso printing services, organize offline workshops, collaborate with creators for consignment sales, provide design services, and host reading groups...
